About Me

I found my love of computer science and programming during my final year studying Chemistry at California Polytechnic Univ. of San Luis Obispo. I started with an intro CPE class and continued on to take the full intro series for the rest of the year. When the pandemic hit, I had time to think about my career goals and decided to switch tracks and use my skills from my intro classes and continue learning through online courses.

I have learned the fundamentals of full-stack web development, including important concepts such as responsive web design, REST API, data structures and algorithms. I've built simple and complex projects using all these new skills. I will continue to further my knowledge and I am excited to have the opportunity to apply what I have learned into real world projects.
